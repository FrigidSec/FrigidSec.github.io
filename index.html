WELCOME TO FRIGIDSEC!

What is FrigidSec?

"By students, for everyone"


FrigidSec is the official cybersecurity community of Vellore Institute of Technology, Bhopal. as established by the department of Cybersecurity and Digital Forensics, school of Computer Science and Engineering in Aug, 2020.
It is also the name of the official CTF team of VIT Bhopal

Why be a part of this community?

You will be able to interact with many other cybersecurity enthusiasts who participate in CTFs, secure coding tournaments, conferences, talks, etc as well as specialize in different areas such as Reverse Engineering, Cryptography, OSINT and many others. You will be able to share and receive knowledge.

The FrigidSec CTF team also regularly releases practice CTF challenges and their solutions so you will be able to improve your skills through regular practice.
